Learn More About The Problem

More than 20,000 elephants are killed each year.

More than 100 elephants are killed every day.

Over the past decade, the elephant population has dropped about 60%. These majestic creatures could be fully extinct by the end of the upcoming decade.

Elephants are killed mostly for their ivory and even food. People find value in their tusks, bones, and meat. How could you possibly want to hurt such a beautiful animal?
